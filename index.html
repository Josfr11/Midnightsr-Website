Our Story

Born Under the Moonlight

Midnight SR began when a group of night riders discovered the magic of empty city streets bathed in moonlight. What started as informal midnight meetups at a Pasig gas station evolved into something greater - a brotherhood bound by the hum of engines and the shared thrill of urban exploration after dark. Our signature midnight rides became legendary, where the city becomes our playground and the stars our navigation points.

More Than Just Rides

We quickly realized our rides could make a difference. Transforming from a riding group to a community force, we organized charity runs that turned our passion into purpose. The "Ride for Hope" events became our proudest tradition, where the roar of our engines translates to help for those in need. From delivering school supplies to remote communities to fundraising for disaster relief, we prove that riders can be angels on two wheels.

A Brotherhood Forged on Asphalt

Today, Midnight SR stands as a testament to what happens when strangers become family through shared miles. Our rides are equal parts adventure and therapy - whether it's the adrenaline of our coastal sprints at dawn or the camaraderie of wrench nights in the garage. We've grown from seven bikes to hundreds, but the feeling remains the same: that magical moment when we all kickstart our engines in unison, ready to write another chapter of our story together on the open road.

Terms & Conditions
1. Membership

All members must complete a 6-month probation period before full membership is granted. During this time, prospective members must attend at least 3 club events and 2 community service activities.

2. Code of Conduct

Members are expected to uphold the highest standards of behavior both on and off the road. Any behavior that brings disrepute to the club may result in disciplinary action up to and including expulsion.

3. Safety Requirements

All riders must wear appropriate safety gear during club rides, including DOT-approved helmets, protective jackets, gloves, and boots. Members must maintain their motorcycles in safe operating condition.

4. Event Participation

Members are encouraged but not required to participate in club events. However, attendance at the annual general meeting is mandatory for all full members.

Privacy Policy
1. Information Collection

We collect personal information including name, contact details, and motorcycle information for membership purposes only. This information is used solely for club administration and event coordination.

2. Data Protection

All personal information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized club officers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access or disclosure.

3. Information Sharing

Member information is never sold or shared with third parties except as required by law or for essential club operations (e.g., event registration with partner organizations).

4. Your Rights

Members may request access to their personal data or request corrections at any time by contacting the club secretary. You may also request deletion of your data upon leaving the club.
