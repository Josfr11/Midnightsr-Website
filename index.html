About Us

About Midnight SR Midnight SR is more than just a motorcycle club its a brotherhood built on respect, loyalty, and the love for the open road. Founded by passionate riders, we believe in the freedom that comes with every mile, the bonds formed on every journey, and the responsibility we carry as part of the riding community.

Who We Are?

We are a group of riders united by our passion for motorcycles and a commitment to supporting one another both on and off the road. Whether it's late-night rides, or just sharing stories over a bonfire, Midnight SR is a family that rides as one.

Mission

Our Mission To promote safe and responsible riding.

To foster strong camaraderie among fellow riders.

To support our community through events and outreach programs.

To uphold the traditions of the motorcycle culture with honor and pride.

Join the Ride!

Join the Ride At Midnight SR, we welcome riders who share our values of brotherhood, respect, and adventure. Whether you ride solo or as part of a crew, if you have the heart of a true rider, you have a place with us. We Ride as One!
