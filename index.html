About Us

Founded in 2024, Midnight SR has grown from a small group of passionate riders to one of the most respected motorcycle clubs in the region.

Our signature midnight rides under the stars have become legendary, giving birth to our name and identity.

Each year, we organize charity rides that have raised over ₱20,000 for local communities.

Who We Are?

We are We are a group of riders united by our passion for motorcycles and a commitment to supporting one another both on and off the road. Whether it's late-night rides, or just sharing stories over a bonfire, Midnight SR is a family that rides as one

Recognized by our black and silver gear with the club's insignia.

Wrench Nights

Our monthly Wrench Nights are where members gather to share mechanical expertise. Beginners learn oil changes and basic maintenance, while advanced riders work on custom mods. Last month we had 15 bikes getting prepped for the season!

  • Every 2nd Thursday
  • JD Works Garage
  • Open to all members
  • Tools provided

Iron Rider

The annual Iron Rider Challenge tests endurance and skill with a 500km ride through mountain passes and coastal roads. Participants must complete the route within 12 hours with only fuel stops allowed.

  • Safety inspection required
  • ₱2,000 entry fee (charity)
  • Starts at 5AM sharp

Coastal Runs

Our famous spontaneous coastal runs begin with a text blast at dawn. Riders meet at the Shell station by 6AM for a mystery route along the most scenic coastal roads. No GPS allowed - follow the leader!

  • Summer months only
  • Breakfast stop included
  • Best sunrises you'll ever see
  • RSVP required

Terms & Conditions
1. Membership

All members must complete a 6-month probation period before full membership is granted. During this time, prospective members must attend at least 3 club events and 2 community service activities.

2. Code of Conduct

Members are expected to uphold the highest standards of behavior both on and off the road. Any behavior that brings disrepute to the club may result in disciplinary action up to and including expulsion.

3. Safety Requirements

All riders must wear appropriate safety gear during club rides, including DOT-approved helmets, protective jackets, gloves, and boots. Members must maintain their motorcycles in safe operating condition.

4. Event Participation

Members are encouraged but not required to participate in club events. However, attendance at the annual general meeting is mandatory for all full members.

Privacy Policy
1. Information Collection

We collect personal information including name, contact details, and motorcycle information for membership purposes only. This information is used solely for club administration and event coordination.

2. Data Protection

All personal information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized club officers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access or disclosure.

3. Information Sharing

Member information is never sold or shared with third parties except as required by law or for essential club operations (e.g., event registration with partner organizations).

4. Your Rights

Members may request access to their personal data or request corrections at any time by contacting the club secretary. You may also request deletion of your data upon leaving the club.
